CE 3372 Water Systems Design

Catalog Description

3372. Water Systems Design (3:3:0). Prerequisite: CE 3305, 3354. Hydraulic analysis and design of municipal water distribution, stormwater collection, and wastewater collection systems. Oral and written presentations. (Communication Intensive)

Design Guidelines - Drinking Water

Lesson 2 examines design manuals as tools to guide hydraulic design of drinking water systems. Manuals from different jurisdictions are examined in the context of a drinking water system design activity.
